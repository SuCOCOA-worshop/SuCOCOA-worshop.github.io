About the Workshop

The HiPC Workshop on Sustainable Compilers Operating Systems and Computer Architecture (SuCOCOA) aims to be the premier academic and industry forum for showcasing sustainability initiatives in the multidisciplinary computer systems research areas spanning hardware, software, and their interaction.

The workshop will highlight research work in the areas and intersection of computer architecture, operating systems and compilers that contribute significantly in upholding one or more of the UN Sustainable Development Goals. The SDGs that will be promoted in the workshop are:

We seek original, high-quality research submissions that improve and further the knowledge of industry-academia in computer systems areas. All types of classical, non-traditional, inter/multi-disciplinary, emerging topics are welcome.
